Our Core Values

These values aren't just posters on walls‚Äîthey guide our decisions, shape our culture, and drive our impact.

Make Work Human

We design compassionate culture and products that prioritize human needs and well-being, both for our employees and our customers.

Manifests in: Flexible work arrangements, mental health support, user-centered design

Be Worthy of Trust

Integrity is our foundation. We protect data, maintain privacy, and build products that users can depend on with their most important work.

Manifests in: Security-first design, transparent communication, ethical decision-making

Own It

We take responsibility from start to finish. When we see a problem, we fix it. When we spot an opportunity, we seize it.

Manifests in: Proactive problem-solving, accountability, initiative-taking

Keep It Simple

Complexity is the enemy of execution. We build products and processes that do a few things really well, eliminating work about work.

Manifests in: Clean product design, streamlined processes, clear communication

They Win, We Win

Success is mutual. When our customers, partners, and teammates succeed, we all succeed. We create win-win situations in everything we do.

Manifests in: Customer-centric decisions, partnership approach, team collaboration

Design for Joy

Even in enterprise software, we infuse delight and play into our work. Creative cultures are more inventive, and joy fuels innovation.

Manifests in: Playful product details, celebratory moments, creative team activities
